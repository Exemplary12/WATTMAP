Calculates Consumer Energy Demand (CED), battery bank sizing, panel array, inverter sizing, and cable sizes for Nigerian solar installations.

// Formulas Used in This Calculator
CED = (Output Energy / 0.9) + Inverter Standby Consumption
Inverter Standby = Total Power × Oversize Factor × 24 × 0.02
Battery Capacity (Ah) = (CED × DoA) ÷ (System Voltage × DoD)
Panel Energy = CED ÷ Performance Ratio (PR = 0.65)
Panel Power = Panel Energy ÷ PSH
No. of Panels = Panel Power ÷ Panel Watt Rating
